Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. The Data We Collect

Dwindl is conceptually designed to expose corporate shrinkflation, not your personal life. When you join our waitlist sequence, we strictly and actively collect:

  • Email Address: Used exclusively to notify you the exact moment the Dwindl mobile application launches to the public.
  • First Name: Harvested to personalize your launch notification and direct communications.
  • Search Telemetry: Anonymous, aggregated search behaviors to help us continually refine our algorithm and identify which products are aggressively shrinking.

2. How We Protect It

Your waitlist payload is isolated inside an encrypted PostgreSQL architecture. We employ strict Row-Level Security (RLS) firewall policies natively at the database level to ensure your personal identifying information is unconditionally cordoned off from public read or write access.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We absolutely do not sell, rent, manipulate, or auction your personal data to third parties. We may utilize highly-secure cloud analytics providers to optimize the speed of the site, but your email and name are sequestered entirely for our internal launch operations.

4. Your Absolute Rights

You have the absolute right to demand the permanent deletion of your waitlist entry at any time. Simply email our operations contact, and your record will be permanently purged from our origin database immediately.

5. Contact Us

If you require any clarification regarding our privacy architecture or data security models, please contact us directly at support@dwindl.ai.
